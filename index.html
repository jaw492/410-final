Project Overview

Elevator Summary: Learn Live is designed by students for students searching for an easier, more efficient way to access new educational content, interactive tutoring, and different practice tools. Learn Live brings these features together into one seamless application. It’s a mobile first interface that feels intuitive for Students who are familiar with modern day technology.

Target Users: Learn Live is designed for middle school, high school, and undergraduate students who want a faster, more engaging learning experience. Students of all ages struggle to stay motivated while learning content for classes. Learn Live is here to help students of all ages succeed in their classes.

Benefits of Learn Live:

  • Streamlined Learning Experience: Lessons, tutoring, and practice tools are all available in one organized interface.
  • Personalized Support: Our AI tutor helps tailor to each student's needs. Offering targeted help for things such as examples and feedback.
  • Accessibility: Students can study anywhere, at any time, with access to help if needed.

Key Features:

  • AI-powered Tutor - This is the core feature of Learn Live. An AI that provides academic support that feels personal. It reduces frustration, saves time, and helps students understand material if a human tutor isn’t available.
  • Personalized Practice and Quizzes - These personalized quizzes help students master topics faster than generic problems sets. Adaptive difficulty ensures students work at their own pace.
  • Real-Time Progress Tracking - Progress indicators keep students motivated. This supports long-term improvement and consistent learning habits.

Evaluation Summary

Process: Our evaluation process consisted of a combination of user-testing with both AI and real world participants. Our methods of gathering feedback on usability testing were by using the USE and SUS scores to evaluate usability, efficiency, and effectiveness. Both AI and human participants were given the same tasks to complete for their respective scoring: log-in, go to the AI chat, navigate to the the quiz section, complete the quiz (regardless of correct or incorrect answer). So, we gave the human participants a time-to-complete of around 5-10 minutes to complete the task they were given. Once the task was completed, the participants would then fill out their USE score or SUS score sheets to report their feedback to us.

Design Changes Made Based on Results:

  • Changes to the main page where the student interacts with LearnLive
  • Sidebar with checkpoints to track activity and doesn’t allow progression to games until reached
  • Notice the quick access to knowledge check(quiz button, Scavenger) This encourages the student to do more activity.

Result Summary

Result: The main design changes were regarding ease of use and ease of learning, with one of the biggest comments being that the buttons were small and in a weird orientation. So, one of the big changes we made was making the buttons larger and in a more intuitive area for the user to quickly cycle through where they wanted to go within the app. Another concern was the lack of interactive learning features and the heavy reliance on AI instead of making a better, more fun way to achieve their studying goals. With that feedback, we decided to implement a more interactive quizzing experience with more questions to test user knowledge, as well as adding a new mode called scavenger mode where users test their knowledge by matching answers with their relevant topics they learned, which greatly improved our user satisfaction regarding interactive learning features.
